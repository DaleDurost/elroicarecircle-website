About Us

Based in Bristol County Massachusetts, El Roi Care Circle is a support group dedicated to caregivers. Named after "El Roi," the biblical term for "the God who sees me," we aim to remind caregivers that they are seen, valued, and not alone. Whether you're caring for a loved one or navigating challenges, we're here to provide encouragement, resources, and a listening ear.
