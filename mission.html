El Roi Care Circle Mission Statement

Our caregiver support group exists to provide a compassionate, faith-inspired community where family caregivers can share their burdens, uncover hidden strengths through listening and mutual encouragement, and find practical and spiritual resources to navigate the challenges of caregiving. Rooted in Christian values of grace, love, and humility, we welcome all who seek connection—regardless of faith background—to bear one another's loads, celebrate small victories, and rely on God's sustaining power, all while respecting privacy and fostering hope for the journey ahead.
